How to play this game

  1. Pick a side — Player 1 always star first
  2. Hit the button roll dice
  3. You can roll as much as you want, the dice score will be added
    to your current score. Until you get dice 1 you will be fine
  4. If you get dice 1, the current score will be lost and became 0.
    The turn will be switched to other player
  5. You can hold the score by hitting hold score, If you do that
    The turn will be switched to other player also
  6. First one who gets 100 and more held scores wins!
